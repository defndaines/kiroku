21 December Weekly Reading Wrap-up

-- Fiction --

“Under the Thumb” is a collection of short stories covering a variety of people affected by their interaction with the police. Every story is tense, and this is not a light read. Though fictional, most of the stories feel all too real. I read this before bed once, then had to commit to reading it during the day, because of how stressful each vignette is. Highly recommended to all.
